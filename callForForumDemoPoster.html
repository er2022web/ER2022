ER FORUM, Demo and Posters

41st International Conference on Conceptual Modeling (ER 2022) Hyderabad, India, October 17-20, 2022

The ER Forum, Demo and Posters track is a vibrant platform for presenting and discussing novel research outcomes that address any topic related to conceptual modelling. Innovation prevails over maturity, in particular regarding emerging new research topics. Thus, the main goal of the track is to facilitate the interaction, discussion and exchange of ideas among presenters and participants.

Author Guidelines

The ER Forum, Demo and Posters track allows for the following types of submissions:

  • Forum papers presenting novel and innovative research outcomes regarding conceptual modeling. Full papers are limited to 14 pages and Short papers have the limit of 8 pages
  • Vision papers are also forum papers, but have a particular focus on futuristic conceptual modeling or anticipate new challenges. Submission of vision papers may describe novel projects that are in an early stage but hold out the strong promise of eventual high impact. The paper should describe what the success criteria are for the vision project. Vision papers have a limit of 4 pages.
  • Tool Demonstrations regard the exhibition of innovative software prototypes or full-fledged systems for developing, communicating and implementing conceptual models. The tools may originate from research initiatives or from industry. Submissions should include a short description including the significance to the conceptual modeling field, the maturity of the work, and if available, a link to a web page or screencast. Here, papers are optional up to 4 pages.
  • Posters should present visionary ideas and/or innovative research projects for which at least some preliminary results are available. We accept works in a relatively early stage. Submissions should include a short description including the significance to the conceptual modeling field and the maturity of the work and if available, a link to a web page. Poster papers have a limit of 4 pages.

The papers will be published in CEUR-WS. The papers must adhere to the new CEUR-ART style for writing papers, 1-column style. You can download an offline version with the style files fromÂ http://ceur-ws.org/Vol-XXX/CEURART.zip (refer to files with 1col postfix).

A paper submitted to ER 2022 Forum, Demo and Posters track must not be under review in any other conference or journal during the time it is being considered for ER 2022. The submissions are due in PDF format through the ER 2022 submission system hosted by EasyChair at https://easychair.org/my/conference?conf=er2022 by selecting the Forum, Demo and Posters track.

Publication and Presentation

All submissions will be reviewed by an international program committee. The volume of accepted ER 2022 Forum, Demo and Poster papers shall be submitted to CEUR-WS.org for online publication.

In order to proper discussion and feedback opportunities, the ER 2022 Forum, Demo and Poster accepted submissions will be presented in an interactive format. Details will be advised later.
